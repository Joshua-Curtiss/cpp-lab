Join the CPP Lab

We are always looking for passionate researchers and students

Join the Computational Clinical Psychology Lab and contribute to cutting-edge research on emotional disorders and affect science! The focus of the lab is using innovative statistical and machine learning strategies to improve the treatment and classification of emotional disorders. Our research emphasizes time-series methods and intensive longitudinal data such as ecological momentary assessment (EMA) of affect and digital passive sensor data.

Graduate Students

We accept graduate students through the Psychology and Applied Psychology programs. Graduate students receive mentored experience in computational clinical psychology with training in both clinical and research skills.

If you are interested in discussing potential opportunities, please contact Dr. Joshua Curtiss at j.curtiss@northeastern.edu

Postdoctoral Researchers

Postdoctoral researchers receive mentored experience in conducting independent and collaborative research projects with emphasis on advanced statistical or computational methods applied to emotional disorders.

Interested candidates should contact Dr. Joshua Curtiss with their CV and research interests.

Research Staff

Research staff positions include research assistants, research coordinators, and lab managers. These positions offer invaluable experience in advanced quantitative methods, time-series data, and independent research on emotional disorders.

Candidates should have a Bachelor's or Master's degree in Psychology, Statistics, or a related field with strong attention to detail and organizational skills.
