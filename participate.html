Research Ethics & Participant Rights

All studies are approved by the Northeastern University Institutional Review Board (IRB). As a research participant, you have the right to:

  • Ask questions about the study at any time
  • Withdraw from the study without penalty
  • Have your data kept confidential
  • Know the results of the research
